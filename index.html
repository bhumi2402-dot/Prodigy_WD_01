    MBBS, D-ORTHO, DNB, TRAUMA & SPINE SPECIALIST

    MBBS, MS, FLAGES & FMAS

    Dr. Chaitanya Patil is a skilled General and Laparoscopic Surgeon at Shree Balaji Lifecare Hospital, Burhanpur. He holds an MBBS, MS (General Surgery), and fellowships in Laparoscopic and Gastrointestinal Surgery (FLAGES) and Minimal Access Surgery (FMAS).     Neuro-Spine Surgeon

    Dr. Swapnil Patil is a skilled Neuro and Spine Surgeon at Shree Balaji Lifecare Hospital, Burhanpur. Dr. Patil is committed to providing precise and patient-focused care with excellent outcomes.
